<p>### <strong>Learning for Life Programme</strong>
</p>
<p>&lt;img src="/images/llp1.jpg" style="width:85%"&gt;</p>
<p><strong>_SPACE_</strong>&amp;nbsp; is a whole school approach to character
building through sports. This is anchored in the beliefs that developing
a passion for sports is an important component in the holistic development
of pupils, and that sportsmen need to possess sound values and character.
Through a 3E approach, various sports module are implemented for all pupils</p>
<p>##### <strong>The Strategic Thrust – Achieving Excellence in Learning &lt;br&gt;3E Approach: Experience, Equip and Excel</strong>
</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_02.JPG)</p>
<p>##### <strong>Pervasiveness of programme&amp;nbsp;SPACE (Experience): Most Valued Pupil (MVP)</strong>
</p>
<p>Pupils learnt RIC3H &amp;amp; WDP Core Values during PE, Art &amp;amp;
Music lessons. Outstanding pupils who displayed &amp;amp; demonstrated
value(s) of the term will be awarded the “MVP award’.</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_03.JPG)</p>
<p>##### <strong>Pervasiveness of Programme&amp;nbsp;SPACE (Experience): Sports for All Pupils</strong>
</p>
<p>**P1: Lower Pri Games Carnival&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>P2: Sports Exposure Programme / Lower Pri Games Carnival&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>P3: Fun Sports / Lower Pri Games Carnival**</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_04.JPG)</p>
<p>##### <strong>Pervasiveness of Programme&lt;br&gt;SPACE (Experience): Sports for all pupils</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>P5: P5 Adventure Camp / Experiential Day</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>&lt;br&gt;Theme: C.A.R.E</strong>
</p>
<p>&lt;br&gt;To collaborate with one another, to adapt to the surroundings
and unexpected changes, to be resilient when faced with challenges and
have empathy to the people around you.</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_05.JPG)</p>
<p>##### <strong>Pervasiveness of Programme&lt;br&gt;SPACE (Experience): Sports for All Pupils</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>P4 - P6: Games Day</strong>
</p>
<p>&lt;br&gt;Pupils develop understanding of movement concepts and skills
acquired in various games setting. Pupils also display the spirit of Olympism
which requires mutual understanding with a spirit of friendship, solidarity
and fair play through the games.&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>Olympic Values: Respect, Friendship, Excellence</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_06.JPG)</p>
<p>#### <strong>Pervasiveness of programme &lt;br&gt;SPACE (Equip): Sports CCAs</strong>
</p>
<p>Pupils demonstrate school core values of respect, resilience, passion,
adaptability, collaboration and innovation as they participate in sports
individually and as a team.&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>![](/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_07.JPG)</p>
<p>#### <strong>Pervasiveness of programme&lt;br&gt;SPACE (Excel): Volleyball Competitions</strong>
</p>
<p>Pupils demonstrate the school motto of ‘Step by Step towards Excellence’
in Zonal and Nationals Competition and develop the ‘Zest to Excel’ attitude
towards competing.&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;</p>
