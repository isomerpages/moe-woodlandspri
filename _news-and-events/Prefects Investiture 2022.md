### **Prefects Investiture 2022**
The theme for Woodlands Primary School's Prefects’ Investiture this year is “Serve with pride, lead with confidence”.

<img src="/images/prefects1.jpg" style="width:65%">

Through Zoom live stream, the Head Prefect and Deputy Head Prefects led the P4 to P6 Prefects in taking their pledge to affirm their commitment to carry out their duties and responsibilities to the best of their abilities.
