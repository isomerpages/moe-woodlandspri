### **Mathematics**
#### **Vision & Mission:**
##### **Vision:**
Woodlanders driven by passion in Math.

##### **Mission:**
We are committed to develop motivated learners with strong Mathematical foundation to stay relevant.

#### **Department Overview:**
The school adopts the MOE Mathematics Curriculum Framework and its Key Focus closely to develop our Woodlanders with the necessary mathematical concepts and skills for continuous learning. It seeks to develop confidence in our Woodlanders and an appreciation of mathematics in their everyday life. Our Woodlanders are given the opportunities for different learning experiences such as hands-on activities as well as the use of technological tools to understand concepts. Our Woodlanders are provided with opportunities to be engaged in thinking, reasoning, communicating ideas and collaborating with one another during their learning process. The school programmes are catered to develop the mass as well as the different groups of pupils as part of our Woodlanders’ learning journey in WDP.

#### **Core Programmes 2022:**
*   Math & Manipulative
*   Factual Fluency & Mental Math
*   Heuristics
*   Fun Maths
*   ICAN Programme
*   LSM and Math Sparks – selected pupils only
*   Math Wizards (P4, P5 and P6) – selected pupils only
