<h3><strong>Learning for Life Programme</strong></h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:85%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/llp1.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong><em>SPACE</em></strong>&nbsp;is a whole school approach to character
building through sports. This is anchored in the beliefs that developing
a passion for sports is an important component in the holistic development
of pupils, and that sportsmen need to possess sound values and character.
Through a 3E approach, various sports module are implemented for all pupils</p>
<h5><strong>The Strategic Thrust – Achieving Excellence in Learning <br>3E Approach: Experience, Equip and Excel</strong></h5>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_02.JPG">
</div>
<h5><strong>Pervasiveness of programme&nbsp;SPACE (Experience): Most Valued Pupil (MVP)</strong></h5>
<p>Pupils learnt RIC3H &amp; WDP Core Values during PE, Art &amp; Music lessons.
Outstanding pupils who displayed &amp; demonstrated value(s) of the term
will be awarded the “MVP award’.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_03.JPG">
</div>
<h5><strong>Pervasiveness of Programme&nbsp;SPACE (Experience): Sports for All Pupils</strong></h5>
<p><strong>P1: Lower Pri Games Carnival<br>P2: Sports Exposure Programme / Lower Pri Games Carnival<br>P3: Fun Sports / Lower Pri Games Carnival</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_04.JPG">
</div>
<h5><strong>Pervasiveness of Programme<br>SPACE (Experience): Sports for all pupils</strong></h5>
<p><strong>P5: P5 Adventure Camp / Experiential Day<br>Theme: C.A.R.E</strong>
<br>To collaborate with one another, to adapt to the surroundings and unexpected
changes, to be resilient when faced with challenges and have empathy to
the people around you.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_05.JPG">
</div>
<h5><strong>Pervasiveness of Programme<br>SPACE (Experience): Sports for All Pupils</strong></h5>
<p><strong>P4 - P6: Games Day</strong>
<br>Pupils develop understanding of movement concepts and skills acquired
in various games setting. Pupils also display the spirit of Olympism which
requires mutual understanding with a spirit of friendship, solidarity and
fair play through the games.
<br>Olympic Values: Respect, Friendship, Excellence</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_06.JPG">
</div>
<h4><strong>Pervasiveness of programme <br>SPACE (Equip): Sports CCAs</strong></h4>
<p>Pupils demonstrate school core values of respect, resilience, passion,
adaptability, collaboration and innovation as they participate in sports
individually and as a team.
<br>
<br>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/LLP%202023/llp2023_07.JPG">

<h4><strong>Pervasiveness of programme<br>SPACE (Excel): Volleyball Competitions</strong></h4>
<p>Pupils demonstrate the school motto of ‘Step by Step towards Excellence’
in Zonal and Nationals Competition and develop the ‘Zest to Excel’ attitude
towards competing.
<br>
<br>
</p>
